About Me

A Passion for Aesthetics & Strategic Brand Growth.

Hello! I am Rouzianah Ndirangu, a seasoned Brand Promoter and Commercial Model based in the vibrant heart of Nanyuki, Kenya. My journey began in the creative arts as a professional model and music video vixen, where I learned the power of visual storytelling and presence.

Today, I leverage that experience to represent globally recognized brands. I have successfully led activations for industry giants like EABL, Windhoek, and Manyatta Liquor. My expertise extends to the retail sector, where I've partnered with Unilever Kenya to drive consumer engagement within local supermarkets.

Currently embracing my "Soft Girl Era," I combine grace with a sharp ambition for marketing. I am dedicated to bridging the gap between premium brands and their target audiences through authentic, high-energy promotion.
